Research

I was a Research Assistant at the Swarthmore Spheromak Experiment, where we create and study plasmas. I worked on studying the autocorrelation function of the magnetic fields inside different plasmas. Check out some of the papers we've published recently.

Nest

I had wonderful summer (2014) interning at Nest. I wrote a post about how my Swat experience contributed to the best internship I've ever had, and how it allowed me to secure a full-time position there for after I graduate.

Sublime & Floobits Tutorial

I wrote a tutorial for CS students wanting to use Sublime Text and/or wanting to program at the same time as their lab partners. It runs you through installation and setup, and will get you running in under 30 minutes.
